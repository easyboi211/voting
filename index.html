How To Vote

  • Log in to your student account using your credentials.
  • Access the "Voting" section on the dashboard menu.
  • Review the candidate profiles and their platforms.
  • Select your preferred candidate for each position by clicking on the corresponding checkbox or button.
  • Double-check your choices and make any necessary adjustments.
  • Click the "Submit Vote" button to finalize your choices.
